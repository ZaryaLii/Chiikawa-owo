Chiikawa

Introduction

Chiikawa (ちいかわ)
also known as Nanka Chiisakute Kawaii Yatsu (なんか小さくてかわいいやつ, "Something Small and Cute"), is a Japanese manga series by Nagano. It has been serialized online via Twitter since January 2020 and has been collected in five tankōbon volumes by Kodansha. An anime television series adaptation by Doga Kobo premiered in April 2022.

Main Story

1.1 Setteing

The world is filled with rich natural scenery such as forests and grasslands, and surrounded by huge food that grows like a plant.
The world is also a civilized place, with homes and stores, a monetary economy and the Internet.

1.2 Chiikawa

The inhabitants of this world, including the Chiikawa, are mainly engaged in jobs such as "weeding" and "hunting" in order to make a living.
These jobs are provided by a race called "armorers", and there are also various qualifications and corresponding exams, depending on the qualification, you can get higher pay, and you can drink alcohol.

1.3 Villain

This world is home to some creepy creatures, some of which can be harmful to Chiikawa. There are also individuals who are capable of having conversations.
